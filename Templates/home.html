an open source musical experience

Somnium (Latin for The Dream) is a high powered machine learning rendition of songs that exist on SoundCloud. Flying from musical cloud to cloud in a virtual culturescape, users will be introduced to different genres of music. We hope to inspire people to appreciate different forms of music, people, ideas, and culture.
