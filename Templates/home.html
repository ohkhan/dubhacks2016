A Cultural Music Escape.

Somnium (Latin for The Dream) is a high powered Machine Learning data set simulation of different genres of songs that exist on SoundCloud. Moving through different bubbles in a virtual world and playing related music along the way, we hope to inspire people to discover and appreciate different forms of music.
